About Me

Builder mindset. Practical execution.

I’m Arunkumar Panchanana, and I’m focused on building useful digital experiences that combine AI, automation, and clear problem-solving.

My approach

I like turning ideas into working tools. For me, the goal is not just to create something interesting, but to build something practical, simple to use, and easy to improve.

I’m especially interested in projects that save time, organize information better, and help users get more value from modern technology.

What I enjoy building

AI tools

Experiences that make AI more useful, more accessible, and more actionable.

Automation workflows

Systems that reduce repetitive work and create smoother day-to-day operations.

Digital products

Simple, focused products with a clear purpose and a clean user experience.

Working philosophy

I believe strong products come from clarity and consistency. Start with a real need, build the simplest version that works, learn from usage, and keep improving.

Small, useful tools can create more impact than large ideas that never get shipped.
